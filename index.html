Take These Essential Steps

1

Do NOT Let Them In

You are not obliged to let anyone into your home, so don't. Once inside, bailiffs have more power.

2

Do NOT Be Intimidated

You do not have to speak to bailiffs and should not respond to intimidation tactics.

3

Do NOT Sign Anything

Never sign anything a bailiff or bailiff company puts in front of you without legal advice.

4

Contact Bailiff Advice

We can stop bailiffs in their tracks and help you write off unaffordable debts.

Types of Debts We Deal With

Council Tax Arrears

If you've missed council tax payments, we can help prevent bailiff action from your local authority.

High Court Enforcement

We handle cases involving High Court Enforcement Officers (HCEOs) enforcing court judgments.

Penalty Charge Notices

PCNs for parking, traffic violations, congestion charges, and other fines.

Utility Arrears

Help with electricity, gas, water, and other utility bill arrears that could lead to bailiff action.

CCJ's

County Court Judgments that affect your credit rating and can lead to enforcement action.

Notice of Enforcement

If you've received a letter saying bailiffs will visit, don't ignore it. We can help.
