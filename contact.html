Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you help me?

We typically respond to inquiries within 1-2 hours during business hours. For urgent cases, call us directly at 0161 826 1292.

Is your advice really free?

Yes, our initial consultation and advice are completely free. We only charge fees if you choose to proceed with our formal mediation services.

Can you stop bailiffs immediately?

In many cases, we can contact bailiffs the same day and request them to pause action while we negotiate with your creditor.

Do you work on weekends?

Yes, we're available Saturday 10am-4pm for phone consultations. We monitor emails throughout the weekend for urgent cases.
